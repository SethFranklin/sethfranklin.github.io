Practicum (STOCKSCH 298G)

The Gardenshare Practicum is fall semester student-taught a 1 credit pass/fail course where you learn the ins and outs of gardening, permaculture, and no-till farming. When the weather permits, the class will be held in the garden, and you will gain hands-on experience planting, harvesting, and organizing the space. When the weather doesn't permit the class to be held outside, you will learn more about permaculture and sustainability indoors. You are allowed to take home any crops that you harvest. The class is taught by amazing students like Shayna!
