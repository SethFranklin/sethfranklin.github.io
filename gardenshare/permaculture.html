Permaculture

What is permaculture?

Permaculture is not a specific technique for cultivating land, but instead a set of ideas or guidelines. It comes from the phrase "permanent culture," and is all about designing land in a way that creates resilient and permanent ecosystems. While a lot of modern enviornment activism surrounds trying to reduce the damage done by modern farming, permaculture tries to work with nature in order to make the ecosystem better and more sustainable. One permaculture technique that Gardenshare employs is no-till farming, which reduces soil erosion.
